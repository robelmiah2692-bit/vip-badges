Account Deletion Policy
We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your account and all associated data from our servers.

How to request account deletion:
To request the deletion of your account and your personal data, please follow these steps:

Send an email to [robelmiah2692@gmail.com] from the email address associated with your account.

Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

Include your Username or User ID in the body of the email.

What data will be deleted:
Once we receive your request, we will permanently remove the following information from our databases within 7 business days:

Your profile information (Name, Profile Picture).

Your chat history and voice room activity data.

Any other personal information associated with your account.

Data Retention:
We do not retain any of your personal data after the deletion process is complete. Please note that once the account is deleted, it cannot be recovered, and you will lose access to all your virtual items, diamonds, and progress.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
